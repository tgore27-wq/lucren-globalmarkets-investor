## 1. Market Summary

Broadcom's after-hours decline from Wednesday cascaded into a full semiconductor sector rout on Thursday. The failure to raise AI chip guidance — despite record quarterly revenue — was read by the market as a potential peak in AI infrastructure spending velocity. AVGO fell approximately 15%, dragging down AMD (-10%), Intel (-9%), Marvell (-14%), and Micron (-8%) in sympathy. The Nasdaq bore the brunt as the SOX index logged its worst single-day performance since the April 2025 tariff panic. Defensive sectors — utilities, healthcare, staples — outperformed as institutional money sought shelter ahead of Friday's critical jobs report.
