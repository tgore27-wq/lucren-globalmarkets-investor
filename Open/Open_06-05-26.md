The jobs print of +172K — more than double consensus estimates — sent Treasury yields surging and triggered violent rotation out of growth stocks into defensives. The semiconductor sector, already weakened by Thursday's Broadcom-led selloff, suffered catastrophic losses as the AI infrastructure cycle peak narrative took hold.
